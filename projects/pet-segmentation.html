Pet Segmentation and Classification

Completed

A multi-task CNN on Oxford-IIIT Pet (37 breeds): shared-encoder foreground/background segmentation plus breed classification, comparing base and Attention U-Net.

Overview

A multi-task CNN on the Oxford-IIIT Pet dataset (7,349 images, 37 breeds): a shared encoder jointly performs binary foreground/background segmentation (from trimap masks) and 37-class breed classification. Base U-Net and Attention U-Net (soft attention gates on skip connections) are implemented and compared. CSE428 — Computer Vision Laboratory, Spring 2026.

Highlights

  • 37-Breed Classification
